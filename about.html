About Niyantri House

Niyantri House is a family-owned restaurant blending Hyderabadi flavors with contemporary dining. Our team crafts comfort foods with a modern twist, using locally sourced ingredients to create a warm, inviting space in Hyderabad.

Hospitality, quality, and a love for bold flavors guide every dish we serve.
